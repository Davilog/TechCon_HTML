History

TechCon started in 2015 as a small meetup for passionate developers in Lagos, Nigeria. Over the years, it grew from a one-day event into an international tech conference, attracting speakers and participants from across the globe. By 2020, TechCon had evolved into a global virtual event, connecting thousands of tech enthusiasts during the pandemic. Today, TechCon 2024 stands as a symbol of innovation and growth in the tech world, bringing together developers, designers, and creators to explore emerging technologies and build the future together.

Mission

Our mission is to foster innovation and collaboration in the tech community by providing a platform for knowledge sharing, networking, and inspiration. We aim to empower developers and tech enthusiasts to push the boundaries of technology and create solutions that make a positive impact on society.
